About C-ADAS

A connected ADAS workflow for detecting, validating, and acting on road hazards.

This platform combines YOLOv8 video analysis, multi-vehicle hazard confirmation, automatic geofence generation, and browser-based alert testing into one cohesive backend and operator dashboard.

Mission

Turn raw detections into actionable road intelligence.

The project is designed for experimentation first, but the architecture already separates ML inference, validation logic, geofence automation, alerts, and dashboard reporting cleanly enough to scale.

Detection

YOLOv8 Video Inference

Uploaded driving videos are processed in the background, metadata is generated, and reports are inserted into the same hazard pipeline used by direct API reporting.

Validation

Multi-Vehicle Proof

Nearby hazard reports from two different vehicle codes convert a pending road issue into a verified hazard, reducing false positives from a single noisy stream.

Response

Automatic Geofence Zones

Once verified, a hazard automatically creates a protective geofence zone so later location checks can trigger warnings with no manual setup.
